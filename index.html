Riley D. Champine

Cartographer, spatial analyst, and graphic designer – i.e., a map geek on the job hunt.

I recently graduated from the University of Oregon with a B.S. in Geography and a minor in Planning, Public Policy & Management. My focuses are Geographic Information Science, cartography, and active transportation advocacy. Right now, I work in Sillicon Valley as a GIS Tech for a big tech company.

I used to be a part of the UO InfoGraphics Lab where I created maps and layouts for an atlas about ungulates in Wyoming and helped maintain a geodatabase of over 80 buildings and 20,000 rooms on campus

Outside of work, I am most likely to be found on a soccer field (refereeing not playing), stubbornly fighting through Bay Area traffic by bike, or playing bass guitar.
