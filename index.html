Riley D. Champine

You've found the website of an aspiring cartographer, spatialist analyst, and graphic designer – i.e., a map geek on the job hunt.

I recently graduated from University of Oregon with a B.S. in Geography and a minor in Planning, Public Policy & Management. My focuses are Geographic Information Science, cartography, and active transportation advocacy. I currently worked in the Geography Department's InfoGraphics Lab where I created maps about ungulates in Wyoming, helped maintain a geodatabase of over 80 buildings and 20,000 rooms, and learned new techniques for understanding and visualizing spatial data.
