Riley D. Champine

I'm an aspiring cartographer, spatialist analyst, and graphic designer i.e., a map geek on the job hunt.


I am a recent graduate from University of Oregon with a B.S. in Geography and a minor in Planning, Public Policy & Management. My focuses are Geographic Information Science, cartography, and active transportation advocacy. I currently work in the Geography Department's InfoGraphics Lab where I create maps about ungulates in Wyoming, help maintain a geodatabase of over 80 buildings and 20,000 rooms, and learn new techniques for understanding and visualizing spatial data.
