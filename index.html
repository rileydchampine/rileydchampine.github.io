Riley D. Champine

Jr. Graphics Editor at Nation Geographic

I'm currently in Washington D.C. working for National Geographic Magazine.

I graduated from the University of Oregon with a B.S. in Geography and a minor in Planning, Public Policy & Management. My academic focuses were Geographic Information Science, cartography, and active transportation advocacy.

I used to be a part of the UO InfoGraphics Lab, where I created maps and graphics for an atlas about ungulates in Wyoming.

Outside of work, I am most likely to be found on a soccer field (often refereeing not playing), stubbornly fighting through District traffic by bike, or playing bass.
